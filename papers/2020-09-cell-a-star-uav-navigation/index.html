Cell a* for navigation of unmanned aerial vehicles in partially-known environments

September 2020 · W Hao, R Wang, A Krolicki, Y Han
  • Paper
  • Code and data
Abstract

This paper presents an extension of A* search and related planners for robust long-distance navigation in partially known environments. The method supports online generation of collision-free and smooth paths with reduced computational burden. It is validated on autonomous drone and remote-control car platforms.
