Virtual Prototyping of Autonomy-Enabled Ground Systems (VIPR-GS)

October 2020 · Wenjian Hao

Project goal: VIPR-GS advances virtual prototyping methods for autonomy-enabled ground systems. The project emphasizes model-based engineering, simulation, and validation workflows to accelerate development and de-risk deployment of autonomous ground platforms.

Funding source: VIPR-GS program (Clemson University-led research initiative).
