I am a researcher interested in developing autonomy for various systems like robotics. I am a Ph.D. candidate from Purdue University School of Aeronautics and Astronautics Engineering. Currently, my research mainly focuses on (1) data-driven dynamics learning and control for complex systems, (2) (distributed) optimization, (3) data-efficient reinforcement learning, and (4) robotics, under the supervision of Dr. Shaoshuai Mou. West Lafayette, IN, USA, 08/2021 - Present. I am mostly interested in using data to learn the dynamics of the unknown or complex nonlinear systems using linear operator techniques, and then develop autonomy using model based control methods like model predictive control, model-based reinforcement learning, sampling-based control, etc.
