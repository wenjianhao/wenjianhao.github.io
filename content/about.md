I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics at Purdue University, advised by Dr. Shaoshuai Mou. My research focuses on learning and control of complex dynamical systems, with particular interests in data-driven dynamics learning using linear operators, data-efficient reinforcement learning, sampling-based optimal control, safety-critical control, and robotics. My work aims to develop computationally efficient and reliable learning-based control algorithms for autonomous robotic systems by integrating machine learning with control theory. I have applied these methods to a variety of robotic platforms, including robotic manipulators, quadrupeds, drones, and autonomous surface vehicles.
