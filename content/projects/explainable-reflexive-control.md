Project goal: RefleXAI is a DARPA-backed effort on adaptive control for uncrewed sea vessels, developed through the Saab and Purdue University collaboration. The project focuses on explainable reflexive control strategies that can respond quickly and robustly in uncertain maritime operating conditions.

Funding source: DARPA (via the Saab and Purdue University collaboration program).

Project page: [Saab and Purdue University team up on DARPA program](https://www.saab.com/markets/united-states/us-newsroom/stories/2024/saab-and-purdue-university-team-up-on-darpa-program-to-advance-adaptive-controls-for-uncrewed-sea-vessels)
