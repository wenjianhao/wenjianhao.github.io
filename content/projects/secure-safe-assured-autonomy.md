Project goal: S2A2 is a NASA University Leadership Initiative project focused on secure and safe assured autonomy. The program develops foundations and practical methods for autonomy that remain reliable, trustworthy, and resilient under safety and cybersecurity constraints.

Project page: [NASA ULI Project 10: S2A2](https://uli.arc.nasa.gov/projects/10/)
